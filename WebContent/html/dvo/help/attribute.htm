Viewing Property Attributes

Search

To view property attributes by searching select one of the three search methods located at the bottom of the page. They are Address Search, PIN Search, and Name Search. Once you have performed a search you will get one of three results.

  1. The search will find a single property and the property attributes are located on the right.


  2. The search will find multiple properties meeting your search criteria. You will then need to select the property from the property attribute area by clicking on the PIN. This will select the property, zoom to it, and list the property attributes.


  3. The search will not find any properties meeting your search criteria.

If you perform a search without getting any properties we advise the Zoom and Select searching technique.

 

Zoom and Select

This technique is particularly useful if you do not know exact information about a property but know the general area of the property. Select the Zoom In tool and click and drag a window on the area that you would like to zoom in to. Continue to do so until you can identify the property you are interested in. Keep in mind that the property boundaries are scale dependent and will not turn on until you are below a scale of 1:15,000.

Once you find the property you are interested in, use the parcel select tool and single left click on the property. Check the property attribute area to see its information.
